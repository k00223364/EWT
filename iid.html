Logo Design - Escape Logo

Another assignment from Interaction Design course in Creative Multimedia, logo design for Escape Festival.

The brief for this assignment was:

'Escape' an exciting new Festival based in the East Clare hills, is looking for a modern, new brand identity to help generate interest in the build up to their summer 2017 launch of their Escape Midsummer Solstice festival. To start with, the client is looking to have a new logo they could use in the initial marketing materials for the Festival.
